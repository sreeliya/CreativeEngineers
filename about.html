About the Company

Creative Engineers, based in Katpadi, Vellore, has been delivering customized water treatment solutions including Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP), Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) and Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems. We handle design, supply, erection and commissioning with a focus on regulatory compliance and sustainability.

We serve industries across chemicals, textiles, food & beverage and commercial sectors, offering turnkey projects and post-commissioning support.
